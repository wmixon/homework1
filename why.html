Cryptomining is not something that people would normally expect that they can do. For the most part it not expected that you will make a lot of money off it all the time but it is a good idea if you want to make some extra cash that would help pay for some of the electronics that you already have.

The effort that is required to mine coins and the amount of effort that is required to learn about crypto coins turns people off almost instantly.

The real thing is that you don't need to know that much about mining if you want to make a simple buck off of it.

All you need to do is download a simple program that you connect to an account such as minergate and click start. Then you can do nothing and make money.
